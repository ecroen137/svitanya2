Booking Information

Svitanya offers workshops as well as concerts.
We will work with you to create the best event for your audience.
Share your needs and ideas with us!

Performance Needs

Contact us to discuss alternatives based on your situation.

  • 10-feet wide by 8-feet deep performance space (minimum). We will stand close together in a concave arc so that we can all see each other.
  • Quality vocal sound system with one microphone for each singer (6-8), plus 2 additional mics for instruments. Please also provide a qualified sound technician to be present before and during the show. Some venues may not require amplification; contact us to discuss.
  • Secure and private backstage space, available starting 2 hours prior to performance, through performance time and for one-hour afterwards.
  • Space suitable for selling CDs, in a well-lighted location obvious and easily accessible to audience before, during and after the performance.
  • When an overnight stay is warranted, double-occupancy hotel rooms are needed.

Fees

These can vary a good deal due to a variety of circumstances. Contact us to discuss.
